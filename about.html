Jammu Genocide Collective FAQ?
What are the goals of Jammu Genocide Collective?

The aim is to collect documents, data and information regarding the Jammu Genocide. To promote awarness of the Genocide along with telling the stories of the victims. To produce research publications, educational material and carrying forward the legacy of the Jammu Genocide.

What type of initiative is Jammu Genocide Collective?

Jammu Genocide Collective is an independent initative, no other organization, govt or institution works or supports this initiative. This is an initiative that aims at raising awareness/recognition of the Muslim genocide committed by Dogra ruler Hari Singh between Oct-Nov 1947 in Jammu region.

How is Jammu Genocide Collective funded?

Jammu Genocide Collective is a completely self funded initative by its members.
