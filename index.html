Dedicated to the awarness and affirmation of the Jammu Genocide

Between Oct-Nov 1947, 200K+ Muslims were systematically mass murdered in Jammu region by mobs and paramilitaries led by the army of Dogra ruler Hari Singh. Multiple massacres were carried out by Dogra state and are commonly known as the Jammu Massacres.
